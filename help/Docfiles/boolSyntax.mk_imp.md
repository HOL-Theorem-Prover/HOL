## `mk_imp` {#boolSyntax.mk_imp}


```
mk_imp : term * term -> term
```



Constructs an implication.


If `t1` and `t2` are terms of type `bool`, then `mk_imp(t1,t2)`
constructs the term `t1 ==> t2`.

### Failure

Fails if `t1` and `t2` are not both of type `bool`.
