## `mk_abs` {#Term.mk_abs}


```
mk_abs : term * term -> term
```



Constructs an abstraction.


`mk_abs (v, t)` returns the lambda abstraction `\v. t`. All free occurrences
of `v` in `t` thereby become bound.

### Failure

Fails if `v` is not a variable.
