## `EVERY_CONSEQ_CONV` {#ConseqConv.EVERY_CONSEQ_CONV}

EVERY_CONSEQ_CONV : (conseq_conv list -> conseq_conv)

Applies in sequence all the consequence conversions in a given list of conversions.
