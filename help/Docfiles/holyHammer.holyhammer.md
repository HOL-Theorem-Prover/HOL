## `holyhammer`

``` hol4
holyHammer.holyhammer : term -> thm
```

------------------------------------------------------------------------

A call to the rule `holyHammer.holyhammer` on a term `tm` is equivalent
to a call to the tactic `holyHammer.hh` on the goal `([],tm)`.
